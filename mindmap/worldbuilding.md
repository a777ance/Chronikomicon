# Worldbuilding & Setting

This page is for the place, the culture, and the rules of the world.

## Setting elements

- Physical geography: city, ruins, wilderness, interior spaces.
- Clock imagery: faces, hands, bell, silence, shadows.
- Temporal feel: cyclical, stagnant, accelerating.

## Cultural elements

- Rituals and observances tied to the clock.
- Language, map, names, symbols.
- Institutions or power structures.

## Anchors

- Scripture anchors and quotations.
- Soundtrack / sensory cues.
- Objects with meaning.

## Rules

- Which rules change between hours?
- What is allowed at 12 but forbidden at 6?
- How do people read or misread the clock?
