# Scene Seeds & Fragments

This page is for small scenes, sparks, and loose fragments.

## Scene ideas

- A clock hand stuck at 6, and the walls refusing to move.
- A character who can hear the hour strike before it happens.
- A ritual that must be completed before the next hour opens.
- A door that only appears at 11 and disappears at 12.

## Fragments

- "The room remembered the hour it had last been entered."
- "She did not count the seconds. She counted the spaces between them."
- "The bell was not loud. It was exact."

## Connection prompts

- What if a backstory is actually a future hour remembered backward?
- Which fragment belongs in the manuscript and which stays in the steam room?
- What image could link two different chapters?
