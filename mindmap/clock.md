# Clock / Dispensations

This page is for the structural heart of Chronikon.

## Central ideas

- 12 hours = 12 dispensations.
- The clock is positional, not a literal time sequence.
- The structure is circular: the end touches the beginning.
- Pivot points: 3, 6, 9, 12.

## Candidate hour textures

- Hour 12: The Call / Beginning / Threshold.
- Hour 1: The First Choice.
- Hour 2: The Weight of the new world.
- Hour 3: Lateral shift / side path.
- Hour 4: Complication / constraint.
- Hour 5: Pause / introspection.
- Hour 6: Nadir / reversal / structural opposite.
- Hour 7: Response / reaction.
- Hour 8: Chase / acceleration.
- Hour 9: Alternate angle / negotiation.
- Hour 10: Preparation / gathering.
- Hour 11: Apex / confrontation.
- Hour 12: Return / echo / restart.
