# Characters & Relationships

This page is for the people and voices of Chronikon.

## Main character node

- Protagonist: who sees the clock, or who is carried by it?
- Core desire: what does this person want?
- Core fear: what would break them?
- Role in the cycle: are they the mover, the witness, the prisoner, the guide?

## Supporting nodes

- Ally / counterpoint
- Antagonist / force of resistance
- Mentor / tradition figure
- Stranger / wild card

## Relationship map

- Protagonist ↔ Ally: trust, betrayal, exchange
- Protagonist ↔ Antagonist: obstacle, reflection, mirror
- Protagonist ↔ World: belonging, exile, transformation
